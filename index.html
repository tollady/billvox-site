FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to download an app? +

Nope. Billvox works over WhatsApp or SMS — channels already on your phone. Nothing to install.

Is GST calculated automatically? +

Yes. Billvox applies 10% GST and formats invoices to ATO standards — labour, materials, subtotal, GST, and total all broken out correctly.

What if I make a mistake in my message? +

Just text a correction. Billvox updates the invoice and resends it. You can also review before it goes out.
